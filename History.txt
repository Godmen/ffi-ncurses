== 0.3.3 / 2010-08-24
* Now compatible with ffi-0.6.3
  - thanks and apologies to all those who requested this change months ago
* Examples now work with both 1.8.7 and 1.9.x
  - for 1.8.6 there you can use
      require "ffi-ncurses/ord-shim"
    to add Integer#ord
* Removed dependency on bones
  - no Rakefile any more - just use gem build ffi-ncurses.gemspec
* Included keydefs.rb by default
